Since the inception of Inline Designs Office Solutions (IDOS) in 1997, we have successfully become a leading provider of furniture systems and services within the Chicago metropolitan market area.  We provide the complete array of services you would expect from a full service dealership.  As a MBE firm, we represent hundreds of complimentary manufacturers of furniture and furnishings to meet our client’s total business needs.  With all of the resources we provide–consultation, project management, design and space planning with computer aided design, warehousing, and installation—we will be a complete source for your firm.  One of the most important services IDOS provides is our thorough knowledge of the products we represent and how to maximize their application.  

Our sales and support staff can help you realize the potential of the products you choose whether it’s by recommending effective configurations, showing you all the choices for colors, materials, and finishes, or informing you of optional features you may not have known about.  When you’ve made your selections, we’ll assist you with the specification process to make your ideas come to life.
